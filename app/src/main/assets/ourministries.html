BBA Village Mission

 

Gospel outreach meetings

Conducting outreach meetings in villages and market places where the people easy to reach and gather. We give more importance for uneducated people to understand God’s word easily and see Christ through the Gospel.. Conducting Convention meetings and Crusades for the society to prove the power of  Holy Spirit. In the end of every meeting Pray for the sick and demon possessed persons.

Making Disciples

Train up the saved persons to follow Christ and live according to the word of God. Mould them Christ like followers. To emphases them to dedicate and live for Christ in all their situations. Train up them to preaching Gospel and conducting worships. Teach them to be a model to their followers. And also insist them to “Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all long suffering and doctrine “ (2.Timothy – 4:2). work for Christ and for helping the needy like widows, chronic disease like cancer, Tuberculosis  and HIV affected people.
